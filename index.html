Like one post from the last 7–14 days. Pick something relevant to the show theme — an AI post for May 20 prospects, a LinkedIn/content post for May 28 prospects. By Day 2 the prospect has seen your name twice.

Day 3
Send Connection Request + personalized note
After two touchpoints, the request feels warm. The note references the show theme — specific, short, no pitch. Under 200 characters. The goal is the connection, not the sale. No links, no attachments.

1st Follow-up — new angle
Different angle from the first DM. Don't repeat the same ask. Add a new hook: the lead data detail (firmographics, live poll intent signals), a social proof reference, or a simpler yes/no question. 2–3 sentences max.
